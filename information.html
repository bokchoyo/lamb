Information

Everything you need to know: format, rules, and schedule.

Registration basics

  • Teams must have 4–6 members.
  • Grades 6–8.
  • One coach (teacher/parent) per school submission.

Contest format

Individual

Short-answer problems. No calculators.

Team

Collaborative set. Calculators allowed.

Guts

Timed sets; submit to unlock next. Live scoreboard.

Rules

  • Allowed: ruler, protractor, compass, non-smartwatch watch.
  • No internet-enabled devices.
  • Scratch paper provided.
