Information

Registration Requirements

  • Teams of 10-12 members
  • Grades 5–8.
  • One coach (teacher/parent) per school

Contest Format

  • All answers are integers between 000-999
  • 3-choice multiple choice (one per digit)
  • AMC/AIME style problems

Individual Round

20 questions
60 minutes

Team Round

10 questions
60 minutes

Rules

  • Allowed: ruler, protractor, compass
  • No internet-enabled devices or calculators
  • Scratch paper provided
